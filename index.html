GUESS THE WORD!

In this game you have to show your knowledge and cleverness to unscramble a word from the scattered letters! Choose the difficulty level, easy - if you don't want to be chased by running out of time, or hard - if you feel confident and want to do it on time! Get five points to win, but if you make five mistakes you lose!

Select a level and start a game!
